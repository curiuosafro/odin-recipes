DO YOU DRINK?

I used to hate the taste of alchol, in-fact I still do.

I wanted to taste that German beer!

I wanted to taste that Russian Vodka!

Most of all I wanted to taste that American Burbon!

My favorite drink to make is the MassCow Mule

It's simple and elegant for those of us who have a strong distrust for jack & coke!

What do you need to make it?

  • A full can of Ginger Beer
  • Two or three or Four Shots of Vodka: I don't judge if you choose to add in a fifth shot
  • Crushed ice:I like mine with crushed ice, like my crushed dreams
  • Freshly squeezed lime-juice
  • Grounded mint: Keep this to a minimum

"How do I make it?" You ask

  1. Grind the ice if you don have a fancy fridge, put it back in the fridge if you don't have everything else ready.
  2. Grind the mint, put it in the firdge until you have everything else ready
  3. Squeeze your lime and make some lime juice, none of that store bought crap
  4. Add your crushed ice to the glass with ground mint
  5. Add generous shots of Vodka to the glass
  6. Add your lime-juice and ginger beer
  7. Stir to mix and serve those ungrateful people
