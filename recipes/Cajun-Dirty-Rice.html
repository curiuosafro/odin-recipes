Lets Make Some Rice!

Why Make This Rice?

What other choice do you have? Once you've tasted this delicous goodness even you grandma will ask you to cook it for her. Life is short, eat your fill. Honestly,

I was tasteless before I ate it, now I can taste. Thank God, it ain't Covid.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups chicken broth (or beef broth)
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper (or a combination of red and green)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, or more to taste I use regular salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice

As they say "One Step Closer" or so

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Add the onions, celery, bell pepper, garlic, salt, pepper, cajun, and cayenne. Stir and continue cooking until the vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add the broth
  4. Add the rice and some more broth and then quickly bring to a boil. Heat the oven to 250 F.
  5. Stir, cover, and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 15 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
  6. Cover the pan and place it in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
  7. Fluff with a fork. Serve and enjoy.
